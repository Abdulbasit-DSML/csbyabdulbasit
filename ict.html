Assessment Overview

Paper 1

Theory

Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes

Weight: 40%

Total Marks: 80 marks

Topics: Questions will be based on sections 1–21 of the subject content

Requirements: All questions are compulsory

Assessment: Externally assessed

Content Overview:
  • 1 Types and components of computer systems

  • 6 ICT applications
  • 7 The systems life cycle

  • 9 Audience
  • 10 Communication
